# Hasalty - Partnership Proposal

## Executive Summary

**Hasalty** is a sophisticated family financial management platform designed to revolutionize how families manage finances together. We are presenting this technology to the National Bank of Egypt as a potential partnership opportunity to enhance customer engagement and financial literacy.

### Key Value Proposition
- **Multi-Platform:** iOS, Android, Web, Windows, macOS, Linux
- **Advanced Analytics:** Weekly, monthly, yearly spending insights
- **Dual-Role System:** Separate parent and child interfaces
- **Production-Ready:** 1,747 lines of well-architected code
- **Modern Tech Stack:** Flutter, Dart, Material Design 3
- **Secure:** MIT licensed, transparent, auditable code
